Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 25, 2026

Spotter respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store and protect your information when using the Spotter mobile application.

Who We Are

Spotter helps users find compatible workout partners based on gym, workout schedule, training goals and fitness level.

Spotter is intended for users who are 18 years of age or older.

Information We Collect

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Gym
  • Workout preferences
  • Training goals
  • Workout schedule
  • Workout posts
  • Messages after matching
  • Reports and blocked users

Firebase Services

Spotter uses Google Firebase including:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Cloud Firestore
  • Firebase Hosting

Google may process certain information according to its Privacy Policy.
